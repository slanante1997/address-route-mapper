About this project

Snap a photo of a handwritten or printed address list and turn it into a ready-to-drive Google Maps route in seconds. A vision model reads the addresses, you review and tidy the list, then open an optimized multi-stop route in one tap.

What inspired it

Built from my experience as a USPS Mail Carrier. USPS' "Package Lookahead" tool is slow and error-prone — This separate tool allows carriers to quickly snap a photo of a route and open it in Google Maps for navigation on their personal mobile device. Google Maps will also provide live notifications to help notify the carrier and reduce the risk of missing a delivery.

Tech stack

  • Angular Standalone components & signals for reactive UI state
  • Gemini API Vision call with structured JSON output to extract addresses
  • Google Maps Provides live directions and notifications for the route
